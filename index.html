21st Century Battle Space Map

A geographical display map of world powers information cards.

Click on each card to get more detailed info.

Each info card will have their rivals and partnerships.

The purpose of this web application is to provide the U.S. American
Citizen information on current world powers and their structures.
It is designed with the 21st Century in mind. With this in mind the user
should not view this with borders in mind, because of the advent of the
internet and international trade. Pay more attention to what
each cards rivals and partners are to better understand the
latest world dynamic.
